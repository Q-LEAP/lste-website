Who we are

The unique testing event in Luxembourg.

The Luxembourg Software Testing Event is a widely acknowledged gathering of Quality Assurance professionals in Luxembourg. The first edition was in 2014. The Luxembourg & Greater Region Testing community have come together to share knowledge, testing practices about the latest trends in software testing. The Luxembourg Software Testing event comprises 3-4 conferences followed by a networking cocktail.

THE BIGGEST TESTING EVENT IN LUXEMBOURG.
FOR ALL TESTERS AND PASSIONATE TESTERS.

For all testers and IT practitioners.

Every year, the entire community of testers and IT practitioners join this event. An event to get valuable insights about testing practices from experts that help enhancing everyday tasks.

Over the years, this conference has gained a reputation of being one of the key and most awaited events on Luxembourg's IT agenda.

Event format

A full day experience.

From an optional morning tutorial to the closing networking cocktail — here's how the day unfolds.

Exhibition area

From 13:00 — meet sponsors, discover tools, explore career opportunities.

Keynote Room

From 13:30 — plenary talks on strategy, AI, automation, and quality culture.

Demo Room

From 14:00 — live technical demonstrations of tools and frameworks.

Networking Cocktail

From 19:00 — structured and informal networking to close the day.

Morning tutorials

An optional hands-on tutorial session runs from 10:00, ahead of the main conference. Spaces are limited — contact us to request the brochure for full details.
