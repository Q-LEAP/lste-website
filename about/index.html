About LSTE

Software quality, taken seriously.

The annual gathering of software testing and QA professionals in the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg.

What is LSTE?

LSTE — Luxembourg Software Testing Event — is the annual gathering of software quality and testing professionals in the Grand Duchy. Now in its 8th edition, it has grown from a small meetup into the region's most anticipated QA event, welcoming 300+ attendees from Luxembourg, Belgium, France, and Germany.

The event features international keynote speakers, hands-on tutorials, live demos, and an exhibition area — all in a single day, free of charge for attendees.

Why software testing matters

Software failures are expensive, embarrassing, and increasingly consequential — in banking, healthcare, and critical infrastructure alike. Testing is how the industry earns trust in the systems it ships. LSTE exists to give that discipline the stage it deserves.

Why Luxembourg

Luxembourg's dense financial-services and tech sector makes software quality a genuinely high-stakes concern, and its size means the testing community here can actually get in one room — which is exactly what LSTE sets out to do, once a year.
