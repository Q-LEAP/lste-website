About LSTE

Luxembourg's Premier Software Testing Event

What is LSTE?

LSTE – Luxembourg Software Testing Event is the annual gathering of software quality and testing professionals in the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg. Now in its 8th edition, LSTE has grown from a small meetup to the region's most anticipated QA event, welcoming over 300 attendees from Luxembourg, Belgium, France, and Germany.

The event features international keynote speakers, hands-on tutorials, live demos, and an exhibition area — all in a single day, free of charge for attendees.

Event Details

  • Date November 26, 2026
  • Time 08:30 – 18:00
  • Venue Terres Rouges Building, Esch-Belval
  • Capacity 300+ attendees
  • Edition 8th edition
  • Admission Free (conference)

Our Mission

We believe that software quality is everyone's responsibility. LSTE creates a space where testers, developers, managers, and executives can learn from world-class experts, share experiences, and build the Luxembourg QA community together.
