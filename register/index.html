Frequently asked questions

Is the conference really free?

Yes — the LSTE conference is always free to attend. Simply register online to confirm your seat. LSTE is made possible by the generosity of our sponsors, who you'll meet at the event.

What does the Tutorial Pass include?

A half-day, hands-on workshop led by an expert practitioner on the morning of the event, capped at 20 participants for an interactive experience. Materials and a certificate of attendance are included.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes. After registering, email hello@lste.lu with your company name, VAT number, and billing address, and we'll issue an invoice promptly.

What's the cancellation policy?

Free tickets can be cancelled any time via your confirmation email. Tutorial passes are fully refundable up to 14 days before the event; after that, transfers to another attendee are possible — contact hello@lste.lu.
