Got questions?

Frequently asked questions.

Is the conference really free?

Yes. The LSTE conference is completely free to attend, for everyone, with no ticket tiers. Simply get your ticket online to confirm your seat. LSTE is made possible by the generosity of our sponsors, who you'll meet at the event.

Can I get an invoice for my ticket?

Tickets are free, so there's nothing to invoice. If your company needs a letter confirming your attendance, email hello@lste.lu.

What's the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your free ticket at any time via your confirmation email, to free up the seat for someone else.
