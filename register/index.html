Join us from 1:00 PM to 9:00 PM for an afternoon and evening of talks, live demos, networking and entertainment. Your free ticket includes access to the full event. Places are limited, so early registration is recommended.

Got questions?

Frequently asked questions.

Is the event really free?

Yes. The event is completely free to attend. There are no ticket tiers, hidden fees or paid upgrades.

What does my ticket include?

Your ticket includes access to all conference sessions, the demo room, exhibition stands, networking areas, refreshments and the evening cocktail reception.

Do I need to register?

Yes. Registration is required because the venue has limited capacity.

Can I attend only part of the event?

Yes. Your ticket remains valid throughout the event, from 1:00 PM to 9:00 PM.

What should I do if I can no longer attend?

Please cancel your registration or contact the event team so your place can be offered to someone else.
