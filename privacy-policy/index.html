1. Who We Are

The LSTE website (lste.lu) is operated by Q-Leap SA, a company incorporated under Luxembourg law, registered at:

Q-Leap SA
10B rue des mérovingiens
L-8070 Bertrange
Luxembourg

Q-Leap SA acts as the data controller for all personal data collected through the lste.lu website. If you have any questions about how we handle your personal data, please contact us at hello@lste.lu.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the LSTE website and communicate with our community. The categories of data we may collect include:

Contact Form Data

When you submit a message via our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

Newsletter Subscriptions

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address only

Subscription is entirely optional and requires your explicit consent. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter email.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Analytics data is collected in anonymised form and does not directly identify individual users. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring websites
  • Browser and device type
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries — When you contact us via the contact form, we use your name and email address to reply to your message.
  • To send the LSTE newsletter — If you have subscribed, we send periodic updates about the LSTE event, speakers, and related news. You may unsubscribe at any time.
  • To improve our website — Anonymised analytics data helps us understand which content is most useful and how we can improve the user experience.

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our processing of personal data is based on the following legal grounds under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Article 6(1)(a) — Consent: For newsletter subscriptions and the use of non-essential cookies (e.g. Google Analytics). You provide explicit consent at the point of subscription or via our cookie settings.
  • Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate Interests: For responding to contact form enquiries. Our legitimate interest is to communicate with individuals who have actively reached out to us regarding the LSTE event.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Contact form data — Retained for a maximum of 2 years from the date of submission, unless an ongoing relationship requires longer retention.
  • Newsletter subscriptions — Retained until you unsubscribe. Upon unsubscription, your email address will be removed from our mailing list within 30 days.
  • Analytics data — Aggregated and anonymised data is retained per Google Analytics standard retention settings (26 months by default).

6. Third Parties & Data Transfers

We work with a small number of trusted third-party service providers to operate this website:

  • Formspree — We use Formspree to process contact form submissions. When you submit a form, your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree's servers. Please review Formspree's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics (operated by Google Ireland Limited) for website analytics. Data is processed in anonymised form. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes beyond LSTE-related communications you have consented to.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of Access — You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure — You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You may object to processing based on legitimate interests. This right is absolute where processing is for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@lste.lu. We will respond within 30 days. There is no charge for exercising your rights. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to collect analytics data.

Cookies We Use

  • Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid, _gat) — These cookies allow us to measure traffic and usage patterns in anonymised form. They do not identify you personally.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out — You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

9. Data Protection Contact

For all data protection enquiries, to exercise your rights, or to raise a concern about our data practices, please contact us:

Email: hello@lste.lu
Post: Q-Leap SA, 10B rue des mérovingiens, L-8070 Bertrange, Luxembourg
Subject line: Data Protection / Privacy Request

10. Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection supervisory authority in Luxembourg:

Commission Nationale pour la Protection des Données (CNPD)
15, Boulevard du Jazz
L-4370 Belvaux
Luxembourg
www.cnpd.lu

If you are located in another EU/EEA member state, you may also contact your local supervisory authority. However, we encourage you to contact us first at hello@lste.lu so we can attempt to resolve any concern directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make changes, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
