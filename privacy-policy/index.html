1. Who we are

The LSTE website (lste.lu) is operated by Q-Leap SA, a company incorporated under Luxembourg law, registered at:

Q-Leap SA
10B rue des mérovingiens
L-8070 Bertrange
Luxembourg

Q-Leap SA acts as the data controller for all personal data collected through this website. Questions about how we handle personal data can be sent to hello@lste.lu.

2. Data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate this website and communicate with our community.

Contact form

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message.

Newsletter

If you subscribe, we collect your email address only. Subscription is optional and requires your consent; you may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

We do not currently run any website analytics or advertising tracking on lste.lu.

3. How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries: we use your name and email to reply to messages sent via the contact form.
  • To send the LSTE newsletter: if you've subscribed, to share event and speaker updates. You may unsubscribe at any time.

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Legal basis for processing

  • Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)): for newsletter subscriptions.
  • Legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)): for responding to contact form enquiries from people who have actively reached out to us.

5. Data retention

  • Contact form data: retained for up to 2 years from submission, unless an ongoing relationship requires longer.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: retained until you unsubscribe; removed within 30 days after.

6. Third parties & data transfers

The Contact page and newsletter signup both use a plain "Email hello@lste.lu" button that opens your own email client — we don't process either through any third-party form service.

Our website includes an optional AI assistant that answers questions about LSTE. If you choose to use it, the messages you type are sent to Google (the Gemini API) to generate a response. Please don't enter personal or sensitive information in the chat. We don't use these conversations for advertising or profiling. See Google's Gemini API terms for details.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not use it for advertising beyond LSTE communications you've consented to.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or port your personal data, to object to processing based on legitimate interests, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@lste.lu; we respond within 30 days, free of charge.

8. Cookies

This website does not use analytics or advertising cookies. Any cookies present are strictly necessary for the site to function (e.g. remembering that you've closed a banner) and require no consent under the ePrivacy rules.

9. Data protection contact

Email: hello@lste.lu
Post: Q-Leap SA, 10B rue des mérovingiens, L-8070 Bertrange, Luxembourg
Subject line: Data Protection / Privacy Request

10. Supervisory authority

You may lodge a complaint with Luxembourg's data protection authority:

Commission Nationale pour la Protection des Données (CNPD)
15, Boulevard du Jazz
L-4370 Belvaux, Luxembourg
www.cnpd.lu

We encourage you to contact us first at hello@lste.lu so we can try to resolve any concern directly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
