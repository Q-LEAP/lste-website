What is LSTE?

Software quality, taken seriously — for one day a year.

LSTE is Luxembourg's annual gathering for software testing and QA professionals. Now in its 8th edition, it has grown from a small meetup into the region's most anticipated testing conference — international keynote speakers, hands-on tutorials, live demos, and an exhibition floor, all in a single day, free of charge.

Venue

Bâtiment des Terres Rouges, Esch-Belval.

Set in Belval's former industrial quarter, the Terres Rouges building pairs Luxembourg's industrial heritage with modern conference facilities — spacious, accessible, and easy to reach by train.

  • 14 Porte de France, 4360 Esch-sur-Alzette
  • Direct train from Luxembourg City (~30 min)
  • Fully wheelchair accessible
