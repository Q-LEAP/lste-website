Why attend

Software quality, taken seriously for one day a year.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is the conference free to attend?

Yes, LSTE is always free to attend. Register online to confirm your seat; it's made possible by our sponsors.

Where can I park?

Free on-site parking is available for guests at the Belle-Vue Conference Center (70 spaces). The venue is also a short walk from the Hamilius tram and bus stop in the heart of Luxembourg City.

What language is the conference held in?

English: all talks, workshops, and event materials are in English.

How do I become a speaker?

Submit a talk through our Become a Speaker page. We welcome proposals from testing and QA professionals at any experience level.

How do I become a sponsor?

Get in touch via our Sponsor With Us page, or see the tiers on our Sponsors & Partners page.

Can students attend?

Yes, students and academic researchers are welcome, free of charge, just like every other attendee. Get your ticket the same way.
