Who we are

The unique testing event in Luxembourg.

Since 2014, LSTE has been where the Luxembourg & Greater Region testing community gathers once a year to compare notes on the craft — a full day of talks and demos, closed out with a networking cocktail. Read our story

Why attend

Five ways to make the most of your one day at LSTE.

LSTE isn't a general tech conference, it's the one day a year Luxembourg's entire QA community gathers in one room: 400+ testers, engineers, and QA leads from banking, insurance, telecom, and the public sector. Here's what a day on the inside looks like.

Keynotes

Sit in on big-picture talks from speakers shaping where testing is headed next.

Workshops

Get hands-on in small-group sessions you can put to work back at the office.

Panels

Hear practitioners debate the calls that don't have easy answers.

Exhibition

Walk the floor and see the tools and platforms testing teams actually use.

Networking

Turn the people in the room into your next go-to contacts, over the closing cocktail.
