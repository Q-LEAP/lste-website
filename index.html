About event

Biggest Testing Event in Luxembourg.

The aim of the Luxembourg Software Testing Event (LSTE) is to gather all the testers, QA and digital practitioners interested in exchanging, learning and networking on the main trends of software testing together. Trends that are at the heart of the changes in IT which translate into a sharp evolution of software testing practices. More info

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Quick answers about LSTE 2026 — the premier software testing conference in Luxembourg.

What is the Luxembourg Software Testing Event (LSTE)?

The LSTE is Luxembourg's largest annual conference for software testing and quality assurance professionals. Now in its 8th edition (2026), it brings together testers, QA engineers, developers, and IT managers from Luxembourg and the Greater Region to share insights on the latest trends in software quality. The full-day event features keynote talks, live demos, an exhibition booth area open throughout the day, and an evening networking cocktail — all free of charge for conference attendees.

When and where does LSTE 2026 take place?

LSTE 2026 takes place on Thursday, 26 November 2026 at the Terres rouges building (14 Porte de France, 4360 Esch-Belval, Luxembourg). Conferences run from 13:00 to 19:00, followed by a networking cocktail from 19:00 onwards. Morning tutorials start at 10:00.

Is the LSTE free to attend?

Yes — access to all keynote sessions, the exhibition booth area, and the evening networking cocktail is completely free. Simply register for a conference ticket on our Ticket page. Morning tutorials (10:00–13:00) are paid workshops at €250 per person, with very limited spots, and include lunch.

How can I submit a speaker proposal for LSTE 2026?

We welcome proposals from practitioners in software testing, QA automation, AI-assisted testing, performance testing, and related fields. The call for speakers is open and submissions close on 30 September 2026. Visit the Call for Speakers page to submit your proposal form.

How can my company become an LSTE sponsor?

LSTE offers four sponsorship tiers: Bronze (€500), Silver (€1,500), Gold (€5,000), and Platinum (custom pricing). All packages include logo placement on the website and event materials, complimentary tickets, and direct access to 300+ QA professionals. Email hello@lste.lu or visit Become a Sponsor for the full package details and sponsorship pack.

How do I get to the Terres rouges venue?

The Terres rouges building is located in Esch-Belval, accessible by tram (line to Belval-Université), train (Esch-sur-Alzette station, 10 min walk), and car (free parking available at the Belval site). Full directions, a map, and parking details are available on the Venue page.
