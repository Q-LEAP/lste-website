Programme 2025

The full schedule from our 7th edition

08:30

Registration & Welcome Coffee

09:00

Welcome & Opening

Nathalie Graindorge, Q-Leap SA
09:15

From Testing to Quality Engineering: The Transformation Journey

Huib Schoots

A deep dive into how organizations need to shift their mindset from testing as a quality gate to engineering quality throughout the entire development lifecycle. Huib draws on his experience coaching dozens of teams across Europe.

10:00

AI in the Testing Trenches: Real-world Experiences

Maxime Moret

Moving beyond the hype, this session presents concrete AI/ML applications in test automation, test case generation, and defect prediction from production deployments.

10:45

Coffee Break & Exhibition

11:15

Continuous Testing in a DevOps World

Thierry Wyns

How to integrate testing seamlessly into CI/CD pipelines without becoming a bottleneck. Practical patterns from large-scale implementations with Xray.

12:00

The Art of Exploratory Testing

Lisi Hocke

Exploratory testing is not random clicking. Lisi shares structured approaches, heuristics, and cognitive models that make exploratory testing a powerful complement to scripted testing.

12:45

Networking Lunch

14:00

BDD That Actually Works

Gojko Adzic

Behavior-Driven Development promises better collaboration but often delivers nothing more than test automation with Gherkin syntax. Gojko explains what it takes to make BDD deliver on its promise.

14:45

Performance Testing at Scale

Bas Dijkstra

When unit tests and integration tests are not enough, performance testing becomes critical. This session covers tools, strategies, and organizational practices for sustained performance testing programs.

15:30

Coffee Break

16:00

Testing in Regulated Industries: Compliance Without Pain

Thierry Soliña

Software testing in regulated sectors (banking, health, government) comes with unique constraints. Thierry shares practical strategies for maintaining quality and speed under compliance requirements.

16:45

The Future of Quality: Panel Discussion

All Speakers

A moderated discussion with all speakers on the future of software quality: AI disruption, the evolving role of the tester, and what skills will matter most in 2030.

17:30

Closing Ceremony & Networking Drinks

09:15

Tool Showcase: Modern Test Management

Xray Team

A live demonstration of modern test management capabilities with Xray, covering test planning, execution tracking, and reporting integrations.

10:00

Live Demo: AI Test Generation

Q-Leap Tech Team

Watch AI-powered test generation in action: from requirements to executable test cases, live on stage with real applications.

11:15

Security Testing Tools Overview

Thales Team

An overview of the security testing landscape: SAST, DAST, and IAST tools demonstrated with real-world vulnerability scenarios.

12:00

Continuous Testing Pipeline Demo

NSI Team

A full walkthrough of a production-grade continuous testing pipeline: from commit trigger through parallel test execution to quality gate enforcement.

14:00

Performance Testing with k6

Sogeti Team

Hands-on demonstration of load and stress testing with k6: scripting, threshold configuration, and integrating results into CI/CD dashboards.

14:45

Test Data Management Best Practices

SQAISuite Team

Managing test data at scale: strategies for data provisioning, masking, synthetic data generation, and maintaining data consistency across environments.

16:00

Automation Frameworks Showdown

Deloitte Team

A comparative head-to-head of leading automation frameworks: Playwright, Cypress, Selenium, and Robot Framework — evaluated across speed, maintainability, and ecosystem maturity.

08:30

Sponsor Booths Open

All day

Gold Sponsor Booths

Deloitte · Q-Leap · NSI · Sogeti

Visit our Gold sponsors' booths throughout the day. Demos, giveaways, and expert conversations available during all breaks and the networking lunch.

All day

Silver Sponsor Booths

AINOS · LNDS · Xray · Thales · Uni.lu · SQAISuite · Tricentis

Explore the Silver sponsors' area for product demonstrations, technical discussions, and networking with industry peers.

All day

Bronze Sponsor Booths

SnT · Q-Guard · jemmic · Q-Bot

Meet our Bronze sponsors and discover innovative tools and services from the Luxembourg and European quality ecosystem.

18:00

Exhibition Closes

08:30

Welcome Coffee & Registration

10:45

Morning Coffee Break

Exhibition Hall
12:45

Networking Lunch

Sponsored by Deloitte

Enjoy a sponsored lunch and take the opportunity to connect with fellow QA professionals, speakers, and sponsors in a relaxed atmosphere.

15:30

Afternoon Coffee Break

17:30

Closing Drinks & Networking

Wind down the day with drinks, canapés, and lively conversations. The perfect moment to exchange contacts and recap the day's insights with the community.

18:30

End of Event
