LSTE 2025 recap

The full programme.

Four tracks, one day — this is the programme from our most recent edition. The 2026 schedule will be published closer to the event.

08:30Registration & Welcome Coffee
09:00
Welcome & OpeningNathalie Graindorge, Q-Leap SA
09:15
From Testing to Quality Engineering: The Transformation JourneyHuib Schoots

A deep dive into how organizations need to shift their mindset from testing as a quality gate to engineering quality throughout the entire development lifecycle.

10:00
AI in the Testing Trenches: Real-world ExperiencesMaxime Moret

Concrete AI/ML applications in test automation, test case generation, and defect prediction from real production deployments.

10:45Coffee Break & Exhibition
11:15
Continuous Testing in a DevOps WorldThierry Wyns

Integrating testing seamlessly into CI/CD pipelines without becoming a bottleneck — practical patterns from large-scale implementations.

12:00
The Art of Exploratory TestingLisi Hocke

Structured approaches, heuristics, and cognitive models that make exploratory testing a powerful complement to scripted testing.

12:45Networking Lunch
14:00
BDD That Actually WorksGojko Adzic

What it actually takes to make Behaviour-Driven Development deliver on its promise of better collaboration.

14:45
Performance Testing at ScaleBas Dijkstra

Tools, strategies, and organisational practices for sustained performance testing programs.

15:30Coffee Break
16:00
Testing in Regulated Industries: Compliance Without PainThierry Soliña

Practical strategies for maintaining quality and speed under compliance requirements in banking, health, and government.

16:45
The Future of Quality: Panel DiscussionAll speakers

A moderated discussion on AI disruption, the evolving role of the tester, and what skills will matter most by 2030.

17:30Closing Ceremony & Networking Drinks
09:15
Tool Showcase: Modern Test ManagementXray Team

A live demonstration of test planning, execution tracking, and reporting with Xray.

10:00
Live Demo: AI Test GenerationQ-Leap Tech Team

AI-powered test generation, live on stage: from requirements to executable test cases.

11:15
Security Testing Tools OverviewThales Team

SAST, DAST, and IAST tools demonstrated with real-world vulnerability scenarios.

12:00
Continuous Testing Pipeline DemoNSI Team

A full walkthrough from commit trigger through parallel test execution to quality gate enforcement.

14:00
Performance Testing with k6Sogeti Team

Scripting, threshold configuration, and integrating load-test results into CI/CD dashboards.

14:45
Test Data Management Best PracticesSQAI Suite Team

Data provisioning, masking, synthetic data generation, and consistency across environments.

16:00
Automation Frameworks ShowdownDeloitte Team

Playwright, Cypress, Selenium, and Robot Framework compared on speed, maintainability, and ecosystem maturity.
