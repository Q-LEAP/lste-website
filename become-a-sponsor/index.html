FAQ

Common questions from sponsors.

What's included in each tier?

Every tier includes logo placement, on-site presence, and a roundtable seat. Gold adds a Silicon Luxembourg interview, lead-scanning, and a booth; Platinum adds a keynote slot and your own hands-on workshop. Get in touch for the full breakdown and pricing.

Do sponsors get a speaking slot?

Yes — Platinum includes a 20-minute keynote in the main auditorium plus a dedicated hands-on workshop, and every tier includes a seat on an expert roundtable panel.

How does lead capture work?

Gold and Platinum sponsors get a badge QR scanner at their booth or workshop entry — scan a badge and get the attendee's name, company, and role instantly.

When will I hear back?

We typically respond to sponsorship enquiries within a couple of business days. Slots are limited, so it's worth reaching out early.

Ready to sponsor LSTE 2026?

Slots are limited — get in touch and we'll send over the full prospectus.
