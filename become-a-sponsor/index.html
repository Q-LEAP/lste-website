Why sponsor LSTE

One event. Multiple channels. Maximum ROI.

Not a general tech event — every attendee is a QA professional: specialists, testers, IT managers, CIOs and CTOs, developers, and digital transformation leaders, from banking, insurance, fintech, telecom, the public sector and beyond. Sponsors get direct exposure to that audience over a full day of booths, keynotes, workshops and networking, plus lead capture and talent sourcing with decision-makers from 60+ organisations.

Get involved

Multiple formats, three tiers.

Sponsors get five ways to be part of the day — a keynote slot, a hands-on workshop, an expert seat on a panel, a booth for live demos, or the evening networking cocktail. Three tiers bundle these together: every tier includes logo placement, on-site presence, and a roundtable seat; Gold adds a Silicon Luxembourg interview, badge QR lead-scanning, and a booth; Platinum adds a 20-minute keynote and your own workshop. Reach also extends beyond the day itself, through our press partner Silicon Luxembourg, our newsletter, and LinkedIn.
