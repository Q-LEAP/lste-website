Frequently Asked Questions

Is the conference really free?

Yes, LSTE conference is always free for all attendees. Simply register online to confirm your spot. There is no catch — LSTE is made possible by the generosity of our sponsors, who you will have the opportunity to meet at the event.

What does the Tutorial Pass include?

A 3-hour deep-dive workshop led by an expert practitioner in the morning before the main conference day. Limited to 20 participants for an interactive learning experience. Workshop materials are provided and you will receive a certificate of attendance.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes. After registration, contact hello@lste.lu with your company name, VAT number, and billing address and we will issue an invoice promptly.

What is the cancellation policy?

Free tickets can be cancelled at any time via your registration confirmation email. Tutorial passes are fully refundable up to 14 days before the event. After that, transfers to another attendee are possible — contact hello@lste.lu to arrange this.
