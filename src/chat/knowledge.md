assistant states about LSTE must come from this document. If something is not

Keep this file up to date when event details change; the chatbot's accuracy

## What LSTE is

- **LSTE** = the **Luxembourg Software Testing Event**, an annual one-day
  conference for software testing and QA (quality assurance) professionals.
- Organised by **Q-Leap SA** (based in Bertrange, Luxembourg).
- It has grown from a small meetup into the region's most anticipated QA event,
  drawing 300+ attendees from Luxembourg, Belgium, France, and Germany.
- The day mixes international keynote speakers, talks, live demos, hands-on
  tutorials/workshops, and a sponsor exhibition — plus networking.

## Tickets & registration

- **Free Conference Ticket** — free. Full access to keynotes, talks, demos, the
  exhibition, and the networking lunch & coffee breaks. Register online.
- **Tutorial Pass** — **€250 + VAT**, one-time payment. A half-day, hands-on
  workshop led by an expert practitioner on the morning of the event, capped at
  20 participants; materials and a certificate of attendance included. For an
  invoice, register first, then email hello@lste.lu with company name, VAT
  number, and billing address.
- **Student & Academic Ticket** — free, but **application required** and a
  **valid student ID** is needed; limited seats. Apply by email when
  registering.
- **How to register:** via the Register page (`/register/`), which links to the
  official registration form hosted on Q-Leap's Odoo:
  https://q-leap.odoo.com/en_GB/event/luxembourg-software-testing-event-2026-24/register
- **Cancellation:** free tickets can be cancelled any time via the confirmation
  email. Tutorial passes are fully refundable up to 14 days before the event;
  after that, a transfer to another attendee is possible — contact
  hello@lste.lu.

## Becoming a sponsor

- LSTE offers sponsorship tiers: Platinium, Gold, and Silver.
- For 2026, Q-Leap (the organiser) is the confirmed Platinium sponsor; Gold
  and Silver are open for other companies to confirm.
- Interested companies can start via the "Sponsor With Us" section
  (`/resources/`) or see current sponsors on the Sponsors & Partners page

## Venue, getting there & practical info

- **Venue:** Conference Center of Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue, 5 Avenue
  Marie-Thérèse, L-2132 Luxembourg (Luxembourg City centre).
- **By tram/bus:** Hamilius (tram & bus) is about a 4-minute walk;
  Luxembourg–Amélie (bus) about 2 minutes. Bus lines 6, 10, 15, 16, and 609
  serve Hamilius.
- **By car:** free on-site parking for guests at the Belle-Vue Conference
  Center (70 spaces, no reservations). Overflow: Parkolux Monterey (5-minute
  walk) or Park & Ride Bouillon via bus 15. Street parking is free after 6 PM.
- **Accessibility:** the venue is **fully wheelchair accessible**, with
  high-speed Wi-Fi throughout and modern conference facilities & breakout
  rooms.
- **Food:** a **networking lunch and coffee breaks are included** with every
  ticket (free ticket included).
- **Hotels nearby:** stay on site at Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue itself, or nearby at
  Hotel Le Royal (city centre) or Melia Luxembourg (Kirchberg, short tram
  ride). See `/venue/` for details.

## Past editions

- LSTE first launched in **2014** and has run **7 editions since then**: 2014,
  2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2024, and 2025. The event was not held in 2015 or
  2020–2023.
- **LSTE 2025** (7th edition): 27 November 2025, Parc Belle-Vue, Luxembourg
  City. Theme: "AI & the Future of Quality." 320+ attendees.
- **LSTE 2024** (6th edition): 14 November 2024, Digital Learning Hub,
  Esch-Belval. Theme: "Continuous Quality." 280+ attendees.
- Recaps and photos: `/previous-editions/` and `/gallery/`.
- LSTE 2026 returns to Parc Belle-Vue in Luxembourg City, the same venue as
  the 2025 edition.
