Internal Mobile Device Farms: Design, Deployment & Long-Term Maintenance in CI

by
Phu X. Mai
-
Quality Assurance Manager at jemmic
.
Mario Willems
-
Technical Lead for SecuChat at jemmic

Keynote by jemmic: Internal Mobile Device Farms: Design, Deployment & Long-Term Maintenance in CI
Presented by Phu X. Mai & Mario Willems

Ensuring robust software quality (QA) for financial clients in banking environments requires precision, security, and scalability. A comprehensive QA process has been developed, covering everything from early story reviews to automated and manual testing, UAT, and security validation. A recent notable innovation has been the design and deployment of internal mobile device farms for Android and iOS testing.

This presentation outlines the journey of designing, deploying, operating, and maintaining these mobile device farms, while comparing the trade-offs between cloud-based solutions such as AWS Device Farm and in-house infrastructure. Attendees will gain insights into architectural decisions, CI/CD integration, and operational challenges faced, as well as the strategies used to overcome them to ensure consistent, secure, and scalable mobile testing. The session provides actionable takeaways for QA professionals looking to improve testing efficiency and reliability.

- A keynote by Phu X. Mai & Mario Willems
14:10 - 14:30

Scaling Mobile Performance Testing with Real Devices and Real Conditions

by
Julien Desmulier
-
Managing Partner, Head of Testing & Business Development, Q-LEAP

Keynote by Q-LEAP: Scaling Mobile Performance Testing with Real Devices and Real Conditions
Presented by Julien Desmulier.

Performance testing on mobile applications goes far beyond running automated scripts. In some cases, achieving realistic results requires controlling the physical positioning of devices, managing network conditions, and ensuring parallel execution across diverse environments. In this talk, I share the challenges and insights gained from a real-world performance testing project where these constraints played a critical role in the validity of the tests. We will explore how Selenium, Appium, and AWS Device Farm were combined to create a scalable, automated, and repeatable performance testing pipeline, and why alternative solutions—including on-site device farms—were evaluated but ultimately discarded. 

Whether you are a QA engineer, test architect, or mobile developer, this talk provides actionable strategies to design performance tests that truly represent how your users experience your mobile app.

Advanced Software Testing of Cyber-physical Systems

by
Prof. Domenico Bianculli
-
Professor, Chief Scientist at the SnT, University of Luxembourg
.
Prof. Fabrizio Pastore
-
Professor, Chief Scientist at the SnT, University of Luxembourg

Keynote by Université de Luxembourg: Advanced Software Testing of Cyber-physical Systems
Presented by Prof. Domenico Bianculli  & Prof. Fabrizio Pastore & Dr. Claudio Mandrioli

Software for safety- and mission-critical systems must be accurately verified to prevent hazards and failures in the field.
This talk presents methods for automatically generating test oracles for cyber-physical systems, assessing the coverage of software test suites, and guiding the generation of missing test cases. It also reports on the application of these methods within research projects conducted in collaboration with industrial partners in the space domain.

— A keynote by Prof. Domenico Bianculli  & Prof. Fabrizio Pastore & Dr. Claudio Mandrioli

Leveraging AI and Data Virtualization for QA in ERP Implementations

by
Dean Bodart
-
Global Partner & Success Manager SQAI-Suite (NSI Partner)

Keynote: Leveraging AI and Data Virtualization for QA in ERP Implementations.
Presented by Dean Bodart, SQAI-Suite (NSI Partner)

This session explores the evolving role of AI in software testing, with a particular focus on ERP implementations and migrations. Led by an experienced quality and test manager, the discussion examines the opportunities and limitations of AI in enhancing quality assurance processes within complex ERP environments, drawing on practical experience. The diversity of business scenarios and ERP solutions makes ensuring quality during such transitions particularly challenging.

A practical example illustrates how AI-driven solutions can automate the generation of contextually appropriate test cases. This approach helps reduce repetitive tasks and improve consistency in test coverage.

The session also addresses the critical issue of test data. The discussion covers a progression from synthetic to semi-synthetic datasets, and ultimately to virtualized databases, which can provide efficiency and scalability benefits depending on the context.

Finally, the session emphasizes that human expertise remains essential. Functional test engineers, automation specialists, and QA managers continue to play a central role in project success. AI is presented as a complementary tool, supporting experienced professionals rather than replacing them.

— A keynote by Dean Bodart, Global Partner & Success Manager SQAI-Suite (NSI Partner)

How Synthetic Data Is Changing the Testing Game

by
Bruno Pacheco
-
Principal Solution Architect at LNDS
.
Silvia Martina
-
Senior Data Engineer at LNDS (Luxembourg National Data Service)

Keynote: How Synthetic Data Is Changing the Testing Game
Presented by Silvia Martina & Bruno Pacheco, LNDS.

Testing software with real data often raises challenges around privacy, compliance, and accessibility. This keynote explores how synthetic data is revolutionizing testing practices by providing safe, scalable, and realistic alternatives. We’ll start by introducing the concept of data as a product — highlighting the importance of quality and preparation for effective testing. Then, we’ll dive into synthetic data: what it is, its benefits, and how it enables robust testing environments without compromising security. Through a practical use case featuring open-source tools like the Synthetic Data Factory Generator, you’ll see how synthetic data can accelerate innovation. Finally, we’ll share resources available in Luxembourg, including repositories and the Open Data Platform, to help you get started. Join us to discover why synthetic data is becoming a cornerstone of modern software testing and quality assurance.

— A keynote by Silvia Martina, Senior Data Engineer & Bruno Pacheco, Principal Solution Architect from LNDS.

by
Avanti Sharma
-
Director of Artificial Intelligence and Growth at Workshop4Me, Founder at Lëtz Make SDGs Happen, Brand Ambassador at WeSTEM+

Data is the lifeblood of Artificial Intelligence, but data quality is the silent factor, unnoticed until it breaks. Join our roundtable, ‘Data Quality: The Unseen Foundation of Reliable AI,’ to explore how high-quality data underpins trustworthy and effective AI systems.

Our discussion brings together leading experts across three key perspectives: the technical side of data standardization and preparation for AI, real-world experiences from organizations navigating the challenges of implementing AI, and finally, the legal and regulatory framework ensuring data compliance. Discover practical insights, learn about best practices, and understand why investing in data quality today is essential for building AI you can truly rely on tomorrow.

Fearless Visibility: Elevating Quality with Xray as Your Integrated Testing Hub

by
Cristiano Cunha
-
Solution Architect & Test Advocate at Xray

DEMO ROOM - Fearless Visibility: Elevating Quality with Xray as Your Integrated Testing Hub

Quality often becomes a black box: distributed across tools, hidden in pipelines, and disconnected from the daily rhythm of the team.

Fearless Visibility introduces how Xraytransforms this landscape by becoming the integrated quality hub directly inside Jira. With Xray, teams can create and manage manual, Cucumber, and generic tests; link them to requirements for complete coverage insights; plan and execute test cycles; and consolidate automated test results from CI/CD pipelines. Instead of searching across multiple systems, stakeholders can now access a single, unified space that provides real-time visibility into the health of their product.

In this talk, you will discover how Xray strengthens traceability, enhances collaboration, and provides clear requirement-to-test-to-execution transparency, helping teams make confident, data-driven decisions. You will also explore how emerging AI capabilities can support quality engineers in their daily workflows, from generating tests to analysing results. Join us to learn how to harness visibility, embrace transparency, and fearlessly elevate the quality of your project.

--- A workshop by Cristiano Cunha, Xray
