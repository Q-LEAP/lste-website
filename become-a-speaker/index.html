FAQ

Common questions from speakers.

Who can submit a talk?

Any software testing or QA professional — practitioners, consultants, researchers, and tool builders are all welcome. First-time speakers are encouraged to apply.

What topics are you looking for?

Test automation, AI in testing, exploratory testing, performance & security testing, agile & DevOps quality practices, test leadership, and anything that helps testers do better work.

Do you cover travel costs?

Yes, reasonable travel and accommodation costs are covered for accepted speakers travelling from outside Luxembourg.

When will I hear back?

Our programme committee responds to every submission within three weeks of the 30 September 2026 deadline.

Ready to submit your talk?

Tell us about your idea — we'll get back to you within three weeks.
