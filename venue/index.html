Venue

Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue, Luxembourg City.

Right in the heart of Luxembourg City, the Conference Center of Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue is minutes from Hamilius by tram, bus, or foot.

About the venue

LSTE 2026 is held at the Conference Center of Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue, right in the heart of Luxembourg City. The venue is easily accessible by public transport, including tram and bus, and for those coming by car, parking is available on-site.

  • 5 Avenue Marie-Thérèse, L-2132 Luxembourg
  • Hamilius (tram & bus), about a 4-minute walk
  • Luxembourg, Amélie (bus), about a 2-minute walk
  • Free on-site parking for guests (70 spaces, no reservations)
  • Fully wheelchair accessible
  • High-speed Wi-Fi throughout the building
Getting there

Coming from outside Luxembourg? Here's the journey.

Whether you're flying in or already in the city, every leg from Findel Airport to the venue door is on free public transport.

Findel

Findel Airport (LUX)

Free bus lines 16 & 29 run directly from the airport to Hamilius, or take the tram (live since March 2025) from the airport stop.

~25 min

Free of charge, all the way

All public transport in Luxembourg has been free since 2020, so no ticket is needed. From elsewhere in the city, bus lines 6, 10, 15, and 609 also serve Hamilius; see mobiliteit.lu for full routes.

4 min

Hamilius → the venue

Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue is a short walk from the Hamilius tram & bus stop, right in the heart of Luxembourg City.

By car

Free parking for guests at the Belle-Vue Conference Center (70 spaces, no reservations): enter the underground garage and turn right. Overflow options: Parkolux Monterey (24b Av. Monterey, 5-minute walk) or Park & Ride Bouillon via bus 15.

Street parking

If both car parks are full, street parking around Luxembourg City is available and free after 6 PM.

Good to know

Venue questions.

How do I get to the venue from Findel Airport?

Take the free bus (lines 16 or 29) or the tram from the airport to Hamilius, about 25 minutes with no ticket needed, and then it's a 4-minute walk to Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the Conference Center of Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue is fully wheelchair accessible.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the building for all attendees.
