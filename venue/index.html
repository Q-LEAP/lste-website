Venue 2026

Terres Rouges Building — Esch-sur-Alzette

Bâtiment des Terres Rouges

LSTE 2026 will be held at the iconic Terres Rouges Building in the heart of the Belval innovation district in Esch-sur-Alzette. This striking venue — set against the backdrop of former blast furnaces — symbolises Luxembourg's transformation from industrial past to a knowledge-driven future.

With its modern conference facilities, spacious networking areas, and excellent transport links, Terres Rouges provides the perfect setting for a day of learning and community.

  • 14 Porte de France, 4360 Esch-sur-Alzette, Luxembourg
  • Easy access by train — Esch-sur-Alzette train station nearby
  • Parking available on site
  • Modern conference facilities & breakout rooms
  • Fully wheelchair accessible
  • High-speed Wi-Fi throughout the building

How to Get There

Terres Rouges is easily reachable by train, car, or bus.

By Train

Luxembourg City to Esch-sur-Alzette takes approximately 30 minutes. Regular services run from Luxembourg City main station throughout the day. From Esch station, the Belval district is easily reachable on foot or by local bus.

By Car

Take the A4 motorway southbound and exit at Esch-Belval. Follow signs for Belval / Porte de France. Free parking is available at the venue and in surrounding car parks.

By Bus

Multiple bus lines serve the Esch-Belval area and connect to Esch-sur-Alzette town centre. Check CFL (tram.lu) for current routes and timetables.
