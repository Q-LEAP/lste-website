Venue

Bâtiment des Terres Rouges, Esch-Belval.

Set against Belval's former blast furnaces, Terres Rouges pairs Luxembourg's industrial heritage with modern conference facilities.

About the venue

LSTE 2026 is held at the iconic Terres Rouges Building in the heart of the Belval innovation district in Esch-sur-Alzette. This striking venue symbolises Luxembourg's transformation from industrial past to knowledge-driven future — modern conference facilities, spacious networking areas, and excellent transport links.

  • 14 Porte de France, 4360 Esch-sur-Alzette, Luxembourg
  • Easy access by train — Esch-sur-Alzette station nearby
  • Free parking available on site
  • Modern conference facilities & breakout rooms
  • Fully wheelchair accessible
  • High-speed Wi-Fi throughout the building
Getting there

Easily reachable by train, car, or bus.

By train

Luxembourg City to Esch-sur-Alzette takes about 30 minutes, with regular services throughout the day. Belval is a short walk or local bus ride from Esch station.

By car

Take the A4 motorway southbound and exit at Esch-Belval, following signs for Belval / Porte de France. Free parking is available at the venue and nearby.

By bus

Multiple lines serve Esch-Belval and connect to Esch-sur-Alzette town centre. Check tram.lu for current routes.
