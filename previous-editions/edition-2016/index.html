2nd Edition, 6 October 2016

#LSTE2016.

The Luxembourg Software Testing Event returned to Forum Da Vinci for an evening of talks and networking on the latest trends in software testing.

About the 2nd edition

LSTE 2016 was an evening event bringing together Luxembourg's testing community for five talks covering the state of software testing, mobile application testing, intrusion/security testing, and how startups approach quality, and was followed by a Q&A and networking cocktail.

The event was an initiative of Q-Leap SA, with the participation of Excellium, Continuous SA, and Hamlet Consulting.

Edition details

  • Date
    6 October 2016
  • Time
    16:30 – 19:00
  • Venue
    Forum Da Vinci, 6 bd Grande-Duchesse Charlotte, L-1330 Luxembourg
  • Talks
    5 sessions + Q&A

Speakers

Who took the stage.

Sylvain Perez

CEO, Q-Leap SA

18+ years in IT, including solution architecture, before becoming CEO of Q-Leap in January 2016. A Test-Driven Development advocate who also teaches programming to children (ages 8–12) with Code Club Luxembourg.

Virginie Messe

Director of Banking & Finance Services, Hamlet Consulting

15+ years of experience as a test manager, mainly in banking environments, with a focus on improving test processes and mentoring teams.

Julien Desmulier

QA & Testing Services Manager, Q-Leap SA

6+ years in software testing, leading Q-Leap's test team with experience across multi-platform, mobile, web, and data-migration testing.

Martin Grandcolas

Intrusion Department's Team Leader, Excellium

8+ years in application security: web application firewalls, security-by-design, and static/dynamic security testing. Also teaches at the University of Lorraine, Télécom Nancy, and Code Hackademy.

Oswald De Riemaecker

Co-Founder, Continuous SA

A Linux/open-source enthusiast and DevOps & agile advocate, with cloud-infrastructure experience spanning conception to production, including TDD, BDD, and continuous delivery practices.

With the participation of Excellium, ContinuousPHP, and ISTQB (Gold Partner).
