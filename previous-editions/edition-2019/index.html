5th Edition, 10 October 2019

#LSTE2019.

150+ registrations from 80 companies gathered at House of Startups for an evening on test automation, digital transformation, risk-based testing, and security.

About the 5th edition

LSTE 2019 gathered the Luxembourg testing community at House of Startups for an evening of talks on test automation, digital transformation, risk-based testing, and offensive security, followed by Q&A and a networking cocktail.

The event was powered by Q-Leap, proudly supported and sponsored by Docler Holding, Neotys, Luxembourg-City Incubator, Hacknowledge, GASQ, and House of Startups.

Edition details

  • Date
    10 October 2019
  • Time
    17:30 – 20:30
  • Venue
    House of Startups, 9 rue du Laboratoire, Luxembourg-city
  • Attendance
    150+ registrations, 80 companies
