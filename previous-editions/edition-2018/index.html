4th Edition, 4 October 2018

#LSTE2018.

120+ attendees from 88 organizations gathered for an evening of talks on non-functional requirements, UX quality, continuous testing, and performance testing.

About the 4th edition

LSTE 2018 brought together the Luxembourg testing community for an evening of talks spanning non-functional requirements, UX quality assurance, continuous testing in agile environments, and performance testing in DevOps pipelines.

The event was powered by Q-Leap, with Docler Holding, Neotys, and Luxair Group as partners and sponsors.
