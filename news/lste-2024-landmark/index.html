LSTE: a landmark event for software testing professionals.

Published 11 December 2024 · Originally published on Silicon Luxembourg

On November 14, 2024, the Luxembourg Software Testing Event (LSTE) held its 6th edition at the Digital Learning Hub in Esch-Belval.

Organized by Q-Leap and partners, the event brought together over 280 professionals, solidifying its role as a key platform for the software testing community in Luxembourg and beyond.

Highlights of the day

The event featured interactive workshops on artificial intelligence, test automation, and digital accessibility, providing innovative insights and practical solutions to industry challenges. Keynote presentations in the afternoon explored themes like test management, AI integration, and automation best practices, sparking lively discussions and engagement from attendees.

Participants also explored technology booths hosted by industry leaders such as Q-Leap, Deloitte, and Sogeti, showcasing cutting-edge solutions. The day ended with a networking cocktail, fostering professional connections and collaboration opportunities.

Looking ahead to 2025

Building on its success, the 7th Luxembourg Software Testing Event (LSTE) was scheduled for November 2025. Interested experts, sponsors, and speakers were encouraged to propose topics or express interest at hello@lste.lu.

As Luxembourg's premier software testing event, the LSTE continues to drive innovation and collaboration in the tech ecosystem.
