q-leap is gathering the software players in Luxembourg.

Published 1 October 2017

On October 5th, q-leap organised the 3rd edition of the Luxembourg Software Testing Event, bringing together testers, developers and IT professionals at Forum Da Vinci in Luxembourg for a day of discussions on mobile automated testing, continuous testing, security testing with bug bounty and IoT, and requirements-driven automated security testing.

A packed afternoon of talks

The agenda ran from 4:30pm onwards, featuring speakers from q-leap SA, IBM, Econocom Digital Security, and the University of Luxembourg's SnT unit. Topics covered mobile testing on iOS and Android, virtualization testing, security testing, and bug bounty challenges — reflecting just how broad the testing discipline had become by 2017.

Becoming a "must-attend" event

As the article put it, LSTE had by then become a "must-attend" event in the Grand-Duchy, offering networking opportunities and free attendance for interested professionals across the Luxembourg tech and IT community.
