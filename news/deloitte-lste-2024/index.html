Deloitte at LSTE 2024: driving innovation in software testing.

Published 11 November 2024

At LSTE 2024, Deloitte shared how it is helping organizations rethink software testing through artificial intelligence and automation, with a particular focus on scaling AI-driven quality practices from proof-of-concept to production.

Testing AI at production scale

Deloitte's session explored the real-world challenges of testing AI components once they move beyond the lab and into live, production environments. As organizations increasingly embed AI into their core systems, traditional testing approaches struggle to keep pace with the non-deterministic, data-driven nature of AI outputs.

Drawing on lessons from real AI deployments, Deloitte highlighted the need for continuous monitoring, robust data quality checks, and testing strategies designed specifically for the unpredictability of machine learning models, rather than treating AI components as just another piece of deterministic software.

From automation to intelligent quality engineering

The session also addressed how AI and automation are reshaping the tester's role. Rather than replacing testers, these tools are shifting the focus toward higher-value activities: designing smarter test strategies, interpreting AI-driven results, and building the guardrails needed to trust automated decisions at scale.

Deloitte's presence at LSTE 2024 reflected its ongoing commitment to advancing software quality practices across Luxembourg's tech ecosystem, sharing practical, field-tested insights with the local testing community.
