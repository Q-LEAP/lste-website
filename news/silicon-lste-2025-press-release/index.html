Press release Silicon – Luxembourg Software Testing Event 2025.

Published 19 November 2025 · Originally published in Silicon Luxembourg

Article published in Silicon Luxembourg.

The Luxembourg Software Testing Event (LSTE) returned on 27 November 2025 at Hôtel Parc Belle-Vue, 5 Avenue Marie-Thérèse, Luxembourg.

One of the IT calendar's most anticipated events

Organised by Q-Leap, this annual conference has established itself as one of the most anticipated events on Luxembourg's IT calendar, bringing together the entire community of testers and IT practitioners. The event serves as a platform for professionals to gather, exchange knowledge, and explore the main trends in software testing that are at the heart of changes in IT and translate into a sharp evolution of software testing practices.

Keynotes, exhibition and a closing cocktail

The conference featured keynote presentations, an exhibition area and a demo room, with a networking cocktail to conclude the day. Attendees gained valuable insights into testing practices from industry experts, with opportunities to explore innovative tools and solutions from the event's sponsors.
