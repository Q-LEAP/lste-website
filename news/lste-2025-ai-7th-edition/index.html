Luxembourg Software Testing Event 2025: AI at the heart of the 7th edition.

Published 19 November 2025

The 7th Luxembourg Software Testing Event (LSTE) returned this year, placing Artificial Intelligence at the center of QA and testing discussions. Organized by Q-Leap, the event brought together IT professionals, testers, and QA experts to explore how AI, automation, and innovative testing practices are shaping the future of software quality.

Keynotes & themes

  • Internal Mobile Device Farms – Optimizing mobile QA for Android & iOS (Phu X. Mai & Mario Willems, Jemmic)
  • Scaling Mobile Performance Testing – Real devices, real conditions, and scalable pipelines (Julien Desmulier, Q-Leap)
  • Advanced Software Testing of Cyber-Physical Systems – Automated oracles and coverage analysis (Prof. Bianculli, Prof. Pastore & Dr. Mandrioli, University of Luxembourg)
  • AI & Data Virtualization for ERP QA – Leveraging AI to enhance test coverage and efficiency (Dean Bodart, SQAI-Suite)
  • Synthetic Data in Testing – Privacy-safe, scalable, and realistic testing (Silvia Martina & Bruno Pacheco, LNDS)
  • Data Quality: Foundation for Reliable AI – Ensuring trustworthy AI through high-quality data (Roundtable moderated by Avanti Sharma)
  • Testing AI Components at Production Scale – Real-world lessons from AI deployments (Liubomyr Bregman, Deloitte)
  • Generative AI in QA – Human-centric insights from two years of AI adoption (Antoine Aymer, Sogeti Group)
  • Beyond Bugs: Building High-Impact QA – Fostering effective, happy QA teams (Réka Balla)

Hands-on labs & demo sessions

  • Fearless Visibility with Xray – Integrated quality hub in Jira (Cristiano Cunha)
  • Q-Guard No-Code Test Automation – Build automated tests without coding (Ritabrata Banerjee & Jonathan Lopez, Q-Leap)
  • Automating E2E & Mobile 2FA – Secure, compliant automation in regulated environments (Jonathan Bernales, Sylvain Perez & João Portugues)

This 7th edition of LSTE highlighted how AI, data quality, and automation are redefining software testing, providing actionable insights for QA professionals across industries.
