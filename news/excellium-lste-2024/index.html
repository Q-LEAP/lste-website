Excellium Services : conférence sur l'intégration des tests et de la sécurité.

Published 12 November 2024

Excellium Services, a key cybersecurity player in Luxembourg, has supported the Luxembourg Software Testing Event since its creation. At LSTE 2024, held on 14 November at the Digital Learning Hub in Esch-Belval, Excellium presented a session titled "Application Testing and Application Security: Overview of the possibilities when we combine both," led by Dominique Righetto.

Bringing security into everyday application testing

The presentation focused on practices for integrating security directly into daily application testing, rather than treating it as a separate, later-stage activity. The goal: help teams catch security issues earlier in the development cycle, alongside the functional checks they're already running.

Meet the Excellium team on-site

Beyond the session itself, attendees were invited to meet the Excellium team at their dedicated booth throughout the day to discuss testing and application security in more depth. Registration for LSTE remained open, with free entry subject to prior reservation at lste.lu.
