Software Testing Event Luxembourg 2016.

Published 5 October 2016 · Originally published in Paperjam

q-leap S.A. announced the 2nd edition of the Software Testing Event Luxembourg, inviting testing professionals to exchange, learn and network on software testing trends. The event promised real-world experiences, new testing tools, and recognition of the testing profession, covering topics including mobile app testing, test automation, security testing, and DevOps.

Five keynote speakers, one shared goal

The event featured five keynote speakers from organisations including Q-Leap, Hamlet Consulting, and Excellium, and was free to attend for software testers, QA professionals, developers, architects, project managers, CTOs and IT managers. Held on 6 October 2016 at 5pm at Forum Da Vinci Luxembourg, it aimed to unite passionate testers and interested professionals around a common goal: sharing practical experience and discovering new testing tools.

Originally published in Paperjam

The announcement was originally published in Paperjam on 14 September 2016, reflecting the event's growing visibility within Luxembourg's broader business press, beyond the testing community's own channels.
